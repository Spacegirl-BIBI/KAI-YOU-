INSOMNIA MC BATTLE

A new MC battle tournament, " INSOMNIA MC BATTLE Vol.1 " (hereinafter referred to as INSOMNIA), will be held in Shibuya on July 19, 2025.

The event was suddenly announced, and it attracted attention with the appearance of not only rappers active in the front line of MC battles such as Lu Bu Karma , Mimei , Sitissy luvit, and Hahanoshikyuu , but also internet talents such as Menchiniki and Ishikawa Noriyuki, who will be participating in an MC battle for the first time. Despite it being the first time, the prize money for the winner is said to be 1 million yen, the same as a large tournament. However, the organizer's information has not been revealed, and the sponsor is an original pack company with no connection to the scene. In short, it is a suspicious event.
